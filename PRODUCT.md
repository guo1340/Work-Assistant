# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Software developers and engineering leads working locally with AI-assisted
delivery pipelines. They need to scan request IDs, stage state, diffs, tests,
risk, approvals, and execution history quickly, often for long sessions and
primarily with a keyboard.

## Product Purpose

DevFlow Assistant is a local-first engineering workflow orchestrator. It makes
multi-agent software work deterministic, inspectable, and traceable from the
verbatim request through implementation, testing, review, and approval. Success
means users can understand what happened, what is blocked, what is risky, and
what requires action without losing context.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, and trustworthy. Linear informs its disciplined grid and crisp
information hierarchy; Vercel informs its restraint, breathing room, and dark
mode; GitHub informs familiar review, diff, and history surfaces.

## Anti-references

Not a marketing surface, playful AI companion, neon cyberpunk console, or
generic card-heavy SaaS dashboard. Avoid decorative gradients, ornamental
motion, oversized hero typography, excessive rounding, and color that does not
communicate state.

## Design Principles

1. Make system state scannable before making the interface expressive.
2. Reveal the evidence behind every status, decision, and approval.
3. Keep dense workflows breathable through hierarchy, alignment, and restraint.
4. Use familiar developer-tool conventions where novelty would slow recognition.
5. Make keyboard and assistive-technology use first-class interaction paths.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve visible focus, semantic status announcements,
keyboard-operable controls, reduced-motion behavior, non-color state cues, and
AA contrast throughout both dark and light themes.
